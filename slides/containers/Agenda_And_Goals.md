# Agenda & Goals

What we'll cover in the next 50 minutes.

---

## Agenda

1. **Containers and IBM Power** — what runs where

2. **What is a container?** — concepts + Podman vs Docker

3. **Running containers** — `podman run` and friends

4. **Building your own images** — Dockerfiles, end-to-end

5. **Lab** — build and run a container you can replay at home

6. **Q&A** — 10 minutes at the end

---

## Goals

By the end of this session you'll be able to:

* Explain **what a container is**, and where they run on IBM Power.

* **Pull and run** a container from a public registry.

* Read and write a **basic `Dockerfile`**.

* **Build an image** and run a container from it on your own machine.

This is a *light* intro — orchestration, networking deep-dives, and
production hardening are out of scope for today.
